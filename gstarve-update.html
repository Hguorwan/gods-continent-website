G-Starve Regional Update

The servers covering Gaza and Palestine regions continue to suffer from critical resource shortages.

Food, loot, and essential object spawns are drastically reduced, leaving many players, including in-game journalists, struggling to survive.

  • Loot spawns are disabled or extremely rare.
  • Hunger mechanics severely impact player health and stamina.
  • Journalist NPCs report starvation status dropping below 5, requiring emergency aid.
  • Resource gathering is currently unsafe due to increased zone instability.

Players are urged to conserve their hunger objects and coordinate with local allies for survival.

⚠️ No official fix date yet. Patch to address this is “maybe” in future updates but remains unconfirmed. Stay alert.
