M2 Client Launch Notice

In response to the 12-hour TikTok ban during patch 1.20.25.1, the developers released a new official video sharing client named M2 (Musical.ly 2).

M2 officially launches with patch 1.20.25.9. All players must manually download the M2 client to regain access to video content features.

  • Legacy TikTok app will stop functioning completely starting in patch 1.20.26.6 or 1.20.26.7.
  • Alternatives such as Chai, Talkie, and PolyBuzz remain viable social options.
  • M2 promises improved video quality, faster load times, and integrated moderation features.
⚠️ Important: Players must ensure timely update to avoid loss of video-sharing functionality.
